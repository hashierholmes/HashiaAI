Privacy Policy

Hashia respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with our bot on Messenger.

1.Information We Collect

When you use Hashia, we may collect:

User Input Data: Messages and commands you send to the bot.

Meta-Data: Time of interaction and chat session details.

Facebook Profile Data: Public profile information shared via Messenger, including name, profile picture, and unique user ID.

2.How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personalized and accurate responses.
  • Improve the bot's functionality and user experience.
  • Monitor for inappropriate or illegal use to ensure compliance with guidelines.

    • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties except as required by law or to comply with Facebook's terms and policies.

    3.Data Retention

    We retain user input data temporarily for processing purposes. However, we do not store data indefinitely. After use, your data is anonymized or deleted within few hours.

    Access and Deletion: You can request access to or deletion of your data by sending an email at hashierholmes.ph@gmail.com or contact the in-charge person Hashier Holmes

    Opt-Out: You can stop interacting with Hashia at any time to cease data collection.

    5.Changes to This Policy
